Bluestone Ranking system

Overview

The Bluestone Ranking System is a formally recognized classification framework established and maintained by the Bluestone community. Progression through the ranks requires active participation and testing within the Bluestone community infrastructure.

Rank Structure

The ranking hierarchy extends from LT5 (lowest tier) to HT1 (highest tier). Players who exceed the maximum ranking threshold are designated as ORS (Over Ranking System) members, representing those who have transcended the standard classification boundaries.

Testing Categories

Advancement through the ranking system is evaluated across four specialized disciplines within Bluestone engineering:

Category Abbreviation Focus Area
Logical Bluestone LO Computational logic systems and digital mechanisms
Farm and Pipeworks FP Automated farming systems and resource pipelines
Piston Doors PD Mechanical door systems and piston-based constructions
Flying Machines and Movestone FM Aerial transportation systems and mobile stone contraptions

Testing Process

Candidates pursuing rank advancement select one or more categories in which they wish to be evaluated. Each test is conducted within the chosen discipline, allowing members to specialize in particular domains or achieve comprehensive mastery across all four categories
